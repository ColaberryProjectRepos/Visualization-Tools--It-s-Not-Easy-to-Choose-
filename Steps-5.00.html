What are the Price range for Power Bi, Qlik and Tableau? 5


        Power BI

• Power BI Desktop is free

• Power BI Pro Share and Collaborate: $9.99 per user per month

• Power BI Premium for Large Deployment: Approx. $24975 per month for 5000 users with 3 nodes    

• Pricing model is subscription and capacity based          

• Custom quote is available depending company needs and types of users



        Qlik:  

•   QlikView Personal Edition: Free (excludes document sharing)

•   QlikView Enterprise edition: $35,000 per server

•   Qlik Sense Cloud Business: $15 per user, per month (billed annually)

•   Qlik Sense Cloud Basic: Free (including sharing up to 5 users)

•   Qlik Sense Desktop: Free (featuring unlimited access)

•   Qlik Sense Enterprise: $33 to $60 per user per month depending on the enterprise size


        Tableau

• Tableau Desktop Personal: $35 per user, per month

• Tableau Desktop Professional: $70 per user, per month

• Tableau Server:  $35 per user, per month

• Tableau Online $42 per user, per month

• Tableau Reader: Free (Read only)

• Tableau Public: Free (public visualization)
