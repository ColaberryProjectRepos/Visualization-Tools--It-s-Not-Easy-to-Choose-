Verdict : Which tool should I choose? 7



Which tool should I choose? The answer is it depends. For this article, we have provided six criteria which are important. More important than picking a single winner is identifying how much weight one should give to each criterion and then examining that criterion in detail. It can be done by looking at what needs must be met in the organization. It is reassuring that all three of the tools have a highly respectable basis level and are future-proof. So, most importantly, one should concentrate most on the criterion that has the greatest weighting for them.
